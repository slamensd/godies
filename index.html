About Us

Our Vision

Welcome to Godies.

We are not just any project, we are a web2 & web3 nft project. Godies is a mythology based nft project which connects and combines one of the oldest greek mythology websites with an nft (non fungible token). Our aim is to build a community full of nft but also mythology enthusiasts. The combination of these two groups is what makes Godies so “unique”.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Go to the OpenSea Menu, then go to Account, Profile and then make sure you are viewing the Collected tab. This is where you can find all your NFTs that you have collected on your specific wallet address.

After the exchange is complete the NFT is transferred into your wallet and appears under the Collected tab on your profile page on OpenSea.

When mint day comes connect your wallet to the minting website. After that enter how many Godies you want to mint and click “Mint Now”. Lastly, confirm the transaction and the NFT will be in your wallet.

To buy and sell NFTs you have to join a marketplace where people showcase, buy, sell and trade NFTs. For example, OpenSea, Rarible, Blur are three of the best marketplaces.
